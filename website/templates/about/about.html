CareerConnect?

At CareerConnect, we specialize in providing jobs, internships and collaboration opportunities to students, tailored to meet their diverse courses. With our inclusion of reputable businesses, our mission is to deliver services that inspires, transforms, and empowers

Our Founding Story

  • 2018
    The journey began with a simple vision: to help students make the most of their knowledge and skills in their respective industries. The company launched with a small but dedicated team, offering skills marketing service through gigs
  • 2019
    As demand grew, the platform expanded to include student-focused project collaborations features. A user-friendly website was introduced, making it easier for all students to collaborate and interact with their peers.
  • 2020
    Despite global challenges, the company thrived by adapting to remote work trends. Many businesses adopted similar operation styles, and this led to our first onboarding of businesses onto the platform
  • 2021
    Freelancer recruitment skyrocketed, and specialized student roles adaptive to different careers became a core service. A dedicated customer support team was established to improve client experience.
  • Present day
    Now a leading job service provider, the company continues to innovate with advanced AI tools, personalized content solutions, and a vast network of professional students. With thousands of satisfied clients, the future looks promising!
